Research

My research combines first-principles modeling and statistical thermodynamics to understand structure–property relationships in high-entropy alloys, Laves phases, and ferroelectric HfO₂ systems. I focus on connecting order–disorder phenomena to macroscopic phase stability and functional behavior.

Techniques include density functional theory (DFT), cluster expansion, Monte Carlo simulations, and data-driven thermodynamic modeling with PyCalphad.
